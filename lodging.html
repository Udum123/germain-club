Heeler's Quiet Comfort Cabins

Heeler’s QUIET COMFORT CABINS is located in the heart of Northern Wisconsin’s famous Vacation Land on 540 acre Lost Lake. With an elevation of almost 1,800 feet, an incomparable climate is found here. The air is crisp and fragrant with a moderate temperature wafted by refreshing pine-laden breezes. It induces rest and peace for the business-weary, assures regained health for the convalescent and acts as a stimulant to young and old alike. We are proud of our resort! We have been in business for over 70 years — one of only a few resorts that are still owned and operated by its original owners. All of our units are either newly built or have been recently updated with new bathrooms, beds, and appliances. We pride ourselves in the cleanest and most up-to-date accommodations in the Northwoods.

Twin Waters Resort

Imagine yourself waking to the beautiful call of a loon. You get up to catch a glimpse of the magnificent sounding creature. As you open the door you see a family of deer dash into the woods. The pure smell of the forest air and sound of complete silence catches your attention. You decide to go to the end of the dock where a bench is positioned. You found it intriguing that it only took a 15 ft. walk to get to the dock from the cabin. As you sat on the bench at the end of the dock, staring out at the beauty of the lake, a massive shadow crosses the water. You look up and see the huge wing span of a Bald Eagle. The eagle soars over your head and lands on an outstretched tree, 500 ft. to the east. Just then a smile comes to your face as you realize you can wake up tomorrow and see it all again.
