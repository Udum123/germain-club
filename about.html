Membership Meetings

General Membership Meetings are held on the second Thursday The time is 7 PM and the location is announced via email to the membership. All members are invited and encouraged to attend. Non-members are invited to attend with the purpose of joining the club.



Board of Director Meetings

Board of Director Meetings are held on the last Thursday of the of the month. These meetings are held at the St. Germain Community Center on Veterans Dr. All members in good standing are invited and encouraged to attend. The Non- Non-members are invited to attend with the purpose of joining. member general public is NOT invited to attend. The Board of Directors meeting is a working meeting of those elected officials The general public is NOT invited to attend these meetings. Members are invited but please do not participate in ongoing discussions. .



Facebook

The St. Germain ATV Club Inc. maintains a Facebook page. The club does not allow individuals to comment or make postings to this page. However, when comments are made we do see them and answer questions as soon as possible. Announcements on the Facebook page are more timely and general in nature than our website and are updated more frequently. Please find us at St. Germain ATV Club. This is the official club page. There are other similar FB pages out there with the name St. Germain ATV Club in their page names, please note the differences. If you need to get in touch with us or have something you'd like to share, you may reach us via email at stgermainatvclub@gmail.com or through a Face Book (FB) private message or notification. If we feel it is appropriate for our page, we will post it and give you the credit for the photo, newsworthy item, or comment.
